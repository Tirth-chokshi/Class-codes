    Simple Sponge Cake

    Ingredients

    • 3 eggs
    • 100g castor sungar
    • 85g self-raising flour

    Notes on Ingredients

        Castor sungar
        Finely granulated white sugar.
    Self-raising flour  
        A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents (usually salt and baking powder)

    Method

    1. Preheat the oven to 190`C.
    2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
    3. In a mdedium bowel, whip together the eggs and castor sugar until fluffy.
    4. Fold in flour.
    5. Pur mixture into the prepared pan.
    6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven , or until the top of the cake springs back when lightlu pressed
    7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
